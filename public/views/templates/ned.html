Eddard Stark

Allegiance: House Stark

Titles: Lord of Winterfell, Warden of the North, Hand of the King

Alias: The Quiet Wolf

Though his family and close friends fondly call him Ned, he is known to most as Eddard Stark, Lord of Winterfell and Warden of the North. His demeanor is both quiet and commanding and he inspires respect in all his fellow Northmen. At the start of A Game of Thrones, he is asked by longtime friend, King Robert Baratheon, to leave his home behind and become Hand of the King to aid Robert in managing a kingdom teetering above a blanket of unrest.
