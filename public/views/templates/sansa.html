Sansa Stark

Allegiance: House Stark

Title: Princess

Alias: Alayne Stone

A girl of 11 at the series' outset, Sansa shows promise in all things expected of a "proper lady" and believes in tales of romance and adventure featuring honest, brave knights. This often leads to friction between her and her sister Arya. At the start of A Game of Thrones, she travels south to the capital with her father Eddard. As she explores more of Westeros, her naivety begins to shed away as she sees a grimmer side to the world.
