Tyrion Lannister

Allegiance: House Lannister

Title: Hand of the King

Alias: The Imp, The Half-Man

A small man can cast a large shadow. Born a dwarf with grotesque facial features, Tyrion endures much disdain and disgust from nearly everyone he comes across. Were it not for his family name, he surely would be treated even worse. Tyrion recognizes his weaknesses and instead plays to his strengths: his sharp wit and his fast tongue. At the start of A Game of Thrones, Tyrion travels north with his family and the King's party. Before returning home, he decides to break from the group and travel North to see the Wall, unaware of the trials, both figurative and literal, that await him.
